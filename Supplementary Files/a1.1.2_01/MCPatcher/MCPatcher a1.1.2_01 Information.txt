MCPatcher (previously known as HD texture fix) is only available starting from version 1.1.6 for Alpha v1.1.2_01.

MCPatcher 1.1.6 and 1.1.7 allows for:
- Animated Water
- Animated Lava
- Animated Fire
- Custom Water
- Custom Lava
- Better Grass

1.1.6 added MrMessiah's Better Grass and custom water and lava animated options.
1.1.7 added "basic support for new update. Lots of bugs."
