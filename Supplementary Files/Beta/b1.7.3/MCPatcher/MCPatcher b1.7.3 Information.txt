MCPatcher_Logan_b173 was found on the Modification Station Discord server.
This mod ports MCPatcher to work with Beta 1.7.3.

MCPatcher b1.7.3 with DepthBufferFix adds the 24bit depth buffer patch to get rid of jaggy lines on clouds, snow and blocks.
This occurs with more modern graphic card drivers that use modern versions of OpenGL.
Mod originally created by Paul Rupe. Port by Vivian (vivianfox. on Discord). Found on the Modification Station Discord server.
